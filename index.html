Our History

Run by Jed Lucan, Black Lantern is the best (and only if you think about it) bar in Haven Springs!

For years we have been offering you the best drinks, snacks and everything else you could possibly want, such as good music and games for you to enjoy.

A cozy atmosphere with lovely waiters (especially newcomer Alex Chen) who will make you feel at home.
